D 96, 022009 (2017) arXiv:1704.04317, however it is the results of a dedicated dark matter analysis with conservative data-quality cuts. D 96, 022009 (2017)   
The background index (7-10keV) is (1.41+-0.22)x10^5 counts / [kg keV day]
